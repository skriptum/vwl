## Mathematik dazu

![img](../images/2023-07-05_10-27-55.jpg)

optimiere: 
$$
C = t * Y(t,A)- A
$$

- *t* = Steuersatz
- *A* = Affluence

$$
\frac{ dC }{dA} = t * \frac{ dY }{dA}- 1 = 0 \\
\frac{ dC }{dt} = y- t \frac{ dy }{dt} = 0
$$
